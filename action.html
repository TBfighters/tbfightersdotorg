Affordable Testing

Danaher, a large biotechnology company, makes some of the best-in-class TB tests, yet they grossly overprice the tests for the people who can least afford it. In 2023, after pressure from TBFighters and others, Danaher promised to sell their basic GeneXpert tests at cost. However, since then, we have seen no proof that their tests truly are at cost. TBFighters are keeping up an active campaign to remind Danaher of these promises and urge them to lower the prices on all of their tests, such as HIV, hepatitis, and many sexually transmitted infections.

Hill Day

TBFighters and partners travel each year to advocate to representatives for increased funding for tuberculosis prevention, testing, and treatment. This is a fantastic opportunity to learn directly from TB experts and survivors, do extremely effective advocacy, and have fun in community with like-minded anti-TB advocates!
