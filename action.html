Affordable Testing

Danaher makes some of the best-in-class TB tests, yet they grossly overprice the tests for the people who can least afford it. In 2023, after pressure from TBFighters and others, Danaher promised to sell their basic GeneXpert tests at cost. However, since then, we have seen no evidence of their promised third-party audit. TBFighters are keeping up an active campaign to remind Danaher of these promises and urge them to lower the prices on all of their tests, not just one.

US Funding Cuts

Many countries, led by the United States, have slashed their foreign aid budgets in recent months. Cuts to USAID, the Global Fund, and PEPFAR have already caused unnecessary death and increased antimicrobial resistance. However, we still have a chance to restore these crucial funds.
