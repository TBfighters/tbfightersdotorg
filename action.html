Take Action

You can join us and help save lives by demanding affordable testing, advocating to protect government global health funding, or educating yourself and others. Affordable Testing

Danaher, a large biotechnology company, makes some of the best-in-class TB tests, yet they grossly overprice the tests for the people who can least afford it. In 2023, after pressure from TBFighters and others, Danaher promised to sell their basic GeneXpert tests at cost. Since then, we have seen no proof that their tests truly are being sold at cost. TBFighters are keeping up an active campaign to remind Danaher of their promises and urge them to lower the prices on all of their tests, such as drug-resistant TB, HIV, hepatitis, and many sexually transmitted infections.

US Funding Cuts

Many countries, led by the United States, have slashed their foreign aid budgets in recent months. Cuts to USAID, the Global Fund, and PEPFAR have already caused unnecessary death and increased antimicrobial resistance. Together we still have a chance to restore these crucial funds.

TBFighters and partners travel to Washington DC each year to advocate to Congressional offices for increased funding for tuberculosis prevention, testing, and treatment.
