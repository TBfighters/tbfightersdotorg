TBReaders Book Club

Welcome to the TBReaders Book Club! Read and discuss books about TB and global health together, with a new book every two months! Below you can find resources to help thoughtfully read books both past and present, and join in TBReaders discussions and events.

Join the club!

We welcome participation across a variety of formats and platforms:

TBReaders Discord Server


Join the TBReaders Discord Server to participate in text-based discussions of our current and past reads, as well as organized voice chat discussions!


Discord invite

Social Media


Pin the TBReaders feed to your home screen on Bluesky and include #TBReaders in your book club–related posts to participate in discussions on that platform, or use #TBReaders accross social media websites to find the TBReaders Book Club community wherever you are!


Bluesky feed

Stay informed!


Follow us on social media, join the TBReaders discord, or subscribe to our newsletter to be informed of special events like author or expert Q&A sessions!
