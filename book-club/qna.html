TBReaders Question & Answer

About the Q&As

For each book we read, we'll schedule a Q&A with the author or another expert to chat with us! Questions will be submitted beforehand and voted on by book club participants.

Previous Q&A's

Everything Is Tuberculosis Q&A Pt. 1

Featuring

John Green: John Green is a young-adult and nonfiction writer, and the author of Everything is Tuberculosis. He is the co-creator of Vlogbrothers, Crash Course, and the Project For Awesome (alongside his brother, Hank.) John is also a member of the board of trustees of Partners in Health, where he (alongside Nerdfighteria) has raised funds to fight maternal mortality in Sierra Leone. He has also advocated for and help fund TB diagnostics, treatment, and prevention around the world.

Everything Is Tuberculosis Q&A Pt. 2

Featuring

Henry Reider: Henry Reider is a content creator, poet, and a MDR-TB survivor from Freetown, Sierra Leone. Through his YouTube channel, Henry shares a window into what life is like in Freetown, including the lifestyle, business, culture, and challenges of his community. Henry is an advocate for improving access to clean water in Sierra Leone, as well as against tuberculosis. His story is told in John Green's Everything is Tuberculosis, a New York Times #1 bestseller.

Phantom Plague Q&A

Featuring

Vidya Krishnan is an Indian journalist, activist, and author of Phantom Plauge. Her work in investigative journalism focuses on health and medical science, covering tuberculosis and the COVID-19 pandemic in India, among other topics. In the past, Vidya has written for The Atlantic, LA Times, The Hindu and Harvard Public Health Magazine.
