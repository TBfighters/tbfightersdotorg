Hill Day Training Sessions Archive

This page serves as an archive for recordings of past training sessions held for TB Hill Day 2025. It will be updated as training sessions pass and recordings become available. If you have any trouble accessing a recording, are missing some other resource, or have any questions, send us an email at tbhillday (at) gmail.com!

TB Advocacy with US Government

In our Hill Day training kickoff on December 14th, experts from Partners In Health covered the continued impact of TB, how Congress writes its budget and makes TB funding decisions, and what our asks will be when we arrive on the Hill in April. Viewing this training session is required to effectively participate in Hill Day 2025!
