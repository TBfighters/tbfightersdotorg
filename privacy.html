TBFighters.org Privacy Policy

Your privacy is of high concern to us. tbfighters.org does not use mechanisms or technologies to gather personal data about its users. No cookies are created, set, or read by the website, and no personal visitor data is gathered at any point. In some cases, certain font, image, video or other resources may be loaded from external sources provided by Google LLC. In these cases, the following data (which is required to ensure functionality), may be provided:

  • Your IP address used to make the request
  • The requested font URL
  • The "User-Agent" header sent by your browser

This data is used for the sole purpose of providing the requested content to your browser.
The data is not used for any other purposes, and is not shared with third parties.
The data is not permanently stored by Google LLC.
The data is not used to create profiles or for targeted advertising.

We may in some cases embed content such as videos directly from other pages such as YouTube. In those cases, you will be provided with an explicit notice that you are about to access content from a third-party provider. This content is not hosted on tbfighters.org servers, and the privacy policy of the third-party provider applies.

In order to gain insight into the usage of the site and reach of the campaign, as well as to detect technical and accessibility issues, we use Clouflare Web Analytics.
Cloudflare Web Analytics does not use any client-side state, such as cookies or localStorage, to collect usage metrics. No fingerprinting of users is performed - in fact, no data tied to any individual user is ever collected.
In order to provide the service, Cloudflare Web Analytics may collect the following information:
- Your IP address, which is not retained by Cloudflare or tbfighers.org and is only used to identify the origin country of the request.
- The URL of the requested resource.
- The User-Agent header sent by your browser.
- The HTTP referrer header.
- Page load time metrics
This data is not associated with any type of identifier and is therefore anonymous.
For more information, please refer to the Cloudflare privacy policy.

For further inquiries and questions, please contact the data privacy officer at legal at tbfighters dot org.
